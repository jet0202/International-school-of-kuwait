Our Flagship Campuses

Our split campuses offer students an American and British inspired education.

Two Campuses, One ISK

A split campus offering a world-class education to all students.

The International School of Kuwait will be based in two locations: the Mahboula campus (opening in September 2020), and the Sabah Al Salem campus (opening in September 2021). Both campuses will be modern, state-of-art facilities offering students the chance to receive what we call, a renaissance education.

Mahboula

Welcoming students to the ISK community in September 2020.

The Mahboula campus of the International School of Kuwait will be located in the city of Mahboula in Kuwait. Mahboula is a brand new 29,400 sq/m facility, scheduled for construction completion in August 2020 and first student acceptance in September 2020.

We will be there through your journey.

At the Mahboula campus there will be a focus on an American inspired curriculum. For AY 2020-21 we will be accepting students between grades KG1 and G10 (age 3.6 years and 15 years by September 1st respectively).

Sabah Al Salem

Officially opening in Sabah Al Salem during September 2021.

The Sabah Al Salem campus of the International School of Kuwait is located in Sabah Al Salem in Kuwait. The campus was first built in 2017 but is being renovated into a 29,500 sq/m facility and scheduled to welcome its first students in September 2021.

A British inspired education with our unique flair.

The Sabah Al Salem campus will be focused on a British inspired curriculum. During AY 2021/22 we will be accepting students for FS1 through to Year 10 (age 3.6 years and 14 years respectively by September 1st).
