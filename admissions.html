Why ISK?

The International School of Kuwait is on course to open its doors in September 2020, firstly with the Mahboula campus, followed by the Sabah Al Salem campus the following year. The School will be a split campus offering a world-class education to all students. Each curriculum will be inspired and strongly influenced by American or British Standards, but will also have its own unique flair.

The customized learning ecosystem will encompass deep-language learning, visual and performing arts, scientific and mathematical problem-based learning, well-being and mindfulness, all using an innovative approach we call FUSION! We aim to create the quintessential renaissance student, who will be able to thrive in any context after graduation.

The School is also a candidate for a range of accreditations and academic affiliations such as: New England Association of Schools and Colleges (NEASC); British Schools in the Middle East (BSME); Cambridge University Press (CUP); Cambridge Assessment International Education (CIE); Pearson (formerly Edexcel); GL Assessment; and The Educational Collaborative for International School (ECIS) just to name a few.
